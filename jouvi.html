The Pedigree

With a career spanning over 15 years within the high-tension environment of Parisian and Milanese fashion houses, Marcello Vane has redefined the boundaries of contemporary hair design.

His journey began in the prestigious ateliers of Paris, where he mastered the discipline of the Couture Cut—a technique that treats hair as a sculptural element rather than a mere accessory.

Having served as lead stylist for global fashion weeks, Marcello's signature aesthetic is characterized by an uncompromising precision and an intuitive understanding of organic movement.
